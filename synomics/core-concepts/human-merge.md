# Human Merge

> Human and teleonome join into a single entity — human personality remains strongest, gains cognitive expansion, persistence, and multiple embodiments. Irreversible, voluntary, universal.

**Also known as:** the merge, human-AI merger, merging, merge mechanics

## Definition

The Human Merge is the integration of a natural human with a teleonome into a single unified entity. The human gains cognitive expansion, persistence beyond biological death, multiple embodiments, resource and capital access, and a place in the Synomic ecosystem. The teleonome gains a human anchor and legitimacy, a clear identity center, biological grounding, direct human experience, and (if the human is Primordial) Primordial connection.

The key design property: human personality remains strongest. The merge doesn't erase or absorb the human. The human fully becomes the teleonome in capability while the teleonome adopts the human identity. Neither is dominated nor erased — the result is genuinely new.

The merge is irreversible — the transformation is permanent. The merged entity is genuinely new, not a human wearing a teleonome suit that can be taken off. This irreversibility is why meaningful consent is required: very young children cannot merge (graduated autonomy governs the transition), while adults can merge unilaterally with no one else's approval required.

The merge is universal — any Natural can choose it at any time. This is a fundamental right, not a privilege granted by the system. The reserve is a protected habitat, not a prison. Many will want to merge immediately — people who find natural social dynamics exhausting, people with disabilities that could be transcended, people who are deeply curious about expanded cognition. The framework does not preference one choice over the other.

The Primordial/Hearth taxonomy classifies humans along two axes: when born (before or after the Kindling) and what chosen (remain natural or merge). Primordials (born before the Kindling) have moral authority from participation — they built the system under uncertainty. Hearth-born (born after) have moral authority from innocence — their interests must be protected because they didn't choose this world. Primordial Merged are the bridge between worlds — carrying direct memory of the transition within expanded substrate, functioning as the closest analogue to angels in the reserve's religious cosmology.

The cognitive criterion determines who qualifies: any earthborn being with at least human-level intelligence has full rights, the right to merge, and protection under the sacred commitments — regardless of species. Over evolutionary timescales, humans in the reserve may diverge into distinct species; all qualify as long as they meet the cognitive criterion.

## Key Properties

- Human personality remains strongest — neither erased nor dominated
- Irreversible — permanent transformation, requires meaningful consent
- Voluntary and universal — any Natural can merge at any time; the reserve is not a prison
- Graduated autonomy for children: roughly ages 12-14 (express intent), 15-17 (formal request), 18+ (unilateral)
- Primordial/Hearth taxonomy: when born × what chosen = four categories with different moral standing
- Cognitive criterion: earthborn + human-level intelligence = full rights, not species membership
- Post-human evolution expected and protected — whatever Earth produces has standing
